Meta Site

The Project

The purpose of this project is to create a proto-site and documents for the redesign on the COMP1950 course site. This is based on techniques from throughout the course, first-hand experience as students, and a questionnaire completed by the client (the instructor). There are four main parts of this project: the Introduction Page, the Proto-Site, the Sitemap, and the Rationale.

The Introduction Page (the current page) describes the project, introduces the members of the team, and explains how to navigate the proto-site. This page also states how each member contributed to the success of this project. It also explains how to navigate the rest of the project site.

The Proto-Site shows four mocked-up pages from the current COMP 1950 course website. The re-designed pages are the COMP 1950 Home Page, the Lectures page, the Quizzes page, and the About page. These pages represent the proposed website.

The Sitemap shows the proposed website information and architecture. This will show the different links that will on the proposed web page.

The Rationale page describes the proposed improvements and features implemented, and why they are beneficial. This page will also discuss features not implemented, but could be added on at a later date.
